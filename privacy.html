Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026.

ASCII to Unicode is designed as a private, browser-based text converter. The core conversion runs locally in your browser and does not require an account.

Local Conversion

Text pasted into the converter is processed in the browser. The site does not need to upload your input, output, or imported TXT file to a server to perform conversion.

Local Storage

The language switch saves your selected language in localStorage so every page can remember your preference. You can clear it from your browser settings.

Analytics Events

We use Google Analytics 4 to measure page views, engagement time, scrolls, outbound clicks, and actions such as convert, mode change, example try, copy, download, import, and language switch. The converter does not send your input, output, or imported TXT content to Google Analytics.

Sensitive Text

Do not paste passwords, private keys, regulated data, or other secrets into any online tool unless you are comfortable processing them in your browser session.
